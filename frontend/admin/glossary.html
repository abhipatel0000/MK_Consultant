Metrics & Jargon Glossary

Find plain-language business explanations for all charts, cards, and terms

Total Views Dashboard

Technical term: Page Views

The total number of times pages on your site were loaded. For example, if a single visitor looks at 3 different service pages, it counts as 3 views.

Unique Visitors Dashboard

Technical term: Distinct Visitors

The number of unique individuals who visited your site. Multiple visits from the same person or device only count as 1 here.

Total Leads Dashboard

Technical term: Form submissions

The total number of contact form submissions received from potential clients who are interested in your services.

Conversion Rate Dashboard

Technical term: Lead Conversion %

The percentage of unique visitors who filled out the contact form and became leads (calculated as Total Leads divided by Unique Visitors multiplied by 100).

WhatsApp Clicks Dashboard

Technical term: WhatsApp Clicks

The number of times visitors clicked your WhatsApp button to start a direct message thread with you.

Call Clicks Dashboard

Technical term: Call Clicks

The number of times visitors clicked your phone icon/link to call your office phone directly.

Email Clicks Dashboard

Technical term: Email Click

The number of times visitors clicked your email link to compose a message.

New Leads Dashboard

Technical term: Active Leads (NEW)

Enquiries with status set to NEW that are brand new and require initial response or operational action.

New Visitors Visitor Traffic

Technical term: New Visitors

People opening your website for the first time on their device during the tracking window.

Returning Visitors Visitor Traffic

Technical term: Returning Visitors

People returning to your website after having visited before at least once.

Total Visits Visitor Traffic

Technical term: Sessions

The total number of browsing sessions (visits) started on your site. A session expires after 30 minutes of inactivity.

Avg Clicks / Visit Visitor Traffic

Technical term: Avg Clicks per Session

On average, how many times a visitor clicked something (like a call button, WhatsApp, pricing plan, or accordion list) during one visit to your site.

Device Viewports Visitor Traffic

Technical term: Device Breakdown

Categorizes what hardware screen size visitors used to browse your site (Mobile phones, Desktop computers, or Tablets).

Referring Sources Visitor Traffic

Technical term: Traffic Referrers

The external domains (such as Google search, Instagram, Facebook, direct link) that visitors came from before landing on your site.

First Page Opened Visitor Traffic

Technical term: Landing Page

The very first webpage a visitor viewed when starting their session. Identifying this helps you know what content hooks them.

Total Interactions Interactions

Technical term: Total Events Logged

The aggregate sum of all clicks recorded on active components, such as contact links, pricing plan details, and documents checklist opens.

Partner Clicks Interactions

Technical term: Partner Clicks

Performance data comparing call, WhatsApp, and email clicks for different team consultant cards.

Document Accordion Engagement Interactions

Technical term: Accordion Open/Close

Tracks which document checklists (e.g. GST requirements, MSME papers) visitors opened on the site to read details.

Service Interest Stats Service Performance

Technical term: Service view metrics

Shows how much traffic, clicks, and conversion rate each offered service category (e.g. MSME, GST, Trademark) receives.

Pricing Views Service Performance

Technical term: Pricing opens

Tracks how many times visitors clicked or expanded the fees and plans section for a specific service.

Service Conversion Rate Service Performance

Technical term: Service Conversion %

The percentage of visitors viewing a service who ended up sending an enquiry form specifically for that service.

Lead Status: NEW Leads

Internal state: NEW

Operational status representing a newly arrived enquiry that has not been read or contacted yet. Requires follow-up action.

Lead Status: CONTACTED Leads

Internal states: CONTACTED / FOLLOW UP / INTERESTED

Operational status indicating you have initiated contact (e.g. called, emailed, or text messaged) and the customer is active in discussion.

Lead Status: CONVERTED Leads

Internal states: IN PROGRESS / DOCS PENDING / COMPLETED

Visual group on the Leads board grouping active clients who are currently working with you (documents pending, task in progress, or completed successfully).

Lead Status: LOST / CLOSED Leads

Internal states: NOT INTERESTED / CLOSED

Visual group on the Leads board grouping leads that did not proceed (Not Interested) or have been finalized and archived.
