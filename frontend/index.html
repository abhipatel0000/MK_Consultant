DOCUMENTATION

Required Documents

Know what documents you need to keep ready before initiating your application process.

01.

GST Registration Requirements

  • Pan Card of Proprietor / Partners
  • Aadhaar Card of Proprietor / Partners
  • Passport Size Photo of Applicant
  • Cancel Cheque or Bank Passbook copy
  • Electricity Bill of Business Place
  • Consent Letter / Rent Agreement (if rented)
02.

MSME Registration Requirements

  • Aadhaar Card of Applicant
  • Pan Card of Business / Proprietor
  • Bank Account Number and IFSC Code
  • Date of Commencement of Business
  • List of Business Activities
03.

FSSAI Food License Requirements

  • Photo of Food Operator
  • Document for Identity proof (Voter ID / Aadhaar)
  • Proof of Possession of Premises (Electricity Bill)
  • Declaration / Food Category List
04.

Trademark Filing Requirements

  • Logo or Brand Name to be filed
  • Signed Power of Attorney (Form TM-48)
  • Registration Certificate (MSME / Partnership)
  • User Affidavit (if trademark is already in use)
